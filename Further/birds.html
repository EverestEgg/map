Field Recording - 23/5/2023 - 12:21pm

The parks have become a popular spot for bird watchers and photographer; a bird hide have been installed to help facilitate these activities. I have certainly seen people wandering around with the massive lenses used for bird photography. Completing this project has shown me why it is so enjoyable.

The lakes were designed as a haven for native and migratory birds, with dead gumtrees installed around the lake specifically for roosting of large water birds. Over 160 bird species have been recorded in the park by the Friends of Newport Lakes since in 1986. With most of them being common native birds, and a few rare sightings.
