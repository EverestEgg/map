Field Recording

Local celebrities, there are two swans the call the lakes home. They are very territorial, and no other swans can make the lakes home due to them. They are often sighted in the lakes, especially around spring were each year they have a few cygnets. Swans work together to rais their chicks, taking turns incubating the eggs and will care for the cygnets for 9 months.

The swans and their babies face many hardships, including foxes who have in the past killed some of the babies, the lack of food due to the poor water quality forcing them to leave the park and look for food in more urban environments, and ending up in dangerous situations.
