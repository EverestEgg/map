Field Recording - 23/5/2025 - 11:45am

This playground is a popular spot for children birthday parties; I have been to and had my fair share of them here. The BBQ’s, covered seating and large amount of open space make it a perfect spot for young children.

This area is called Pavey Park and is less suited to being the natural oasis that the Lakes area has become. Like the lakes, it used to be a quarry called Pavey's Hole. Getting repurposed into Pavey’s Tip in 1975 till 1982, making subsequent revitalisation harder. It was one of the first areas rehabilitated and in 1989 it was opened as Pavey park becoming a traditional grassy, playground area you see today. Though recently small areas of native planning have been done.
